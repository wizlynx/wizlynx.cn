What is Corporate Social Responsibility?

"Corporate social responsibility” is a company’s sense of responsibility towards the community and environment (both ecological and social) in which it operates. Companies express this citizenship through their waste and pollution reduction processes, by contributing educational and social programs, and by earning adequate returns on the employed resources.

How We Contribute

WLX commits to repaying their communities by developing pollution reducing projects and contributing educational programs. The first CSR program, from our WLX China office, is an environmental-friendly bag made of canvas. WLX encourages people to utilize reusable bags made of environmental-friendly material instead of plastic bags. Moreover, this bag is made without a dying process to reduce environmental pollution.
